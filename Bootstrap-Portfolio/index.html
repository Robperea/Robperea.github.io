About Me

My name is Robert Perea-Martinez, and I am currently a student at the Columbia Engineering Coding Bootcamp. I am enjoying the challenge as well as the opportunity to become a Fullstack Javascript Developer.

I love to learn, and I love to solve problems, fortunately, with this program my hands are more than full. I decided to learn software development as a way to exercise my creative ability and to be able to create vehicles one day that can solve the world's problems.

My work experience has been varied, from several years of working in the hospitality industry, to serving as a Logistics Specialist in the US Navy. Since the service, I've been pursuing my undergraduate degree in Industrial/Organizational Psychology at Baruch College and have also been working as a Personal Trainer for the last 5 years.
